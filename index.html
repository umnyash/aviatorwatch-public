Discover the watchmaking behind

  • Luxuriously patterned dial

    The AVIATOR Douglas Day Date 41 dial features a subtle 3D pattern with volumetric markings and an elegantly curved window indicating the day. Since visibility and readability are key attributes, the Douglas Day Date 41 collection unites Art Deco design and function – from bold perfectly polished Roman numerals to the two elevated instrumental rings with luminous indexes that are inspired from the cockpit. With its deep and fine grooves, the pattern of the dial recreates stunning color.
